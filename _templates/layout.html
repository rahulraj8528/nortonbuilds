How to Install Norton with an Existing Product Key via www.norton.com/setup or my.norton.com

Norton is one of the most trusted antivirus and cybersecurity solutions in the world. If you’ve already purchased a Norton product and have a valid product key, installing or reinstalling it on your device is a straightforward process. This guide will walk you through how to install Norton with an existing product key using either www.norton.com/setup or my.norton.com.

Whether you’re performing a fresh install, switching devices, or need to reinstall Norton with a product key, the steps below will help ensure your device is protected in just minutes.

Guide to Install My Norton on This Device: Step-by-Step Setup Using Your Norton Product Key With Ease

Step 1: Prepare Your Norton Product Key

Before starting the installation, make sure you have the following:

  • A valid Norton product key (usually 25 characters)
  • A Norton account (if you don’t have one, you’ll be prompted to create it)
  • A stable internet connection

Your product key can be found in your Norton retail card, confirmation email (if purchased online), or on the packaging.

Step 2: Visit the Official Norton Setup Page

To begin the installation, go to either of the following websites:

  • www.norton.com/setup
  • https www.norton.com setup
  • my.norton.com home setup

These official Norton URLs will redirect you to the Norton sign-in page. You’ll need to log in using your Norton account credentials.

Step 3: Sign In or Create a Norton Account

Once you're on the site:

  • Click on Sign In
  • Enter your email and password
  • If you don't have an account, click Create Account and fill out the required details

Having an account allows you to manage your Norton subscriptions and devices from one central dashboard.

Step 4: Enter Your Norton Product Key

After signing in:

  • Look for the option to enter Norton product key
  • Input your 25-character product key exactly as it appears
  • Click Submit or Next

This step will link your product key to your Norton account, allowing you to proceed with the installation.

Step 5: Download and Install Norton on Your Device

Once your product key is accepted:

  1. Click on Download to start downloading the Norton installer
  2. Run the downloaded file and follow the on-screen instructions
  3. The installer will check for existing Norton versions, uninstall them if needed, and begin a fresh installation

If you're trying to install My Norton on this device after uninstalling it previously, this process acts as a Norton reinstall with product key.

Step 6: Complete Setup and Run First Scan

Once installation is complete:

  • Open Norton
  • Allow it to update virus definitions
  • Run your first system scan to ensure your device is secure

Congratulations! You’ve successfully completed the Norton download install with product key process.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: Can I use the same Norton product key on multiple devices?

It depends on the subscription plan. Some Norton products allow protection for up to 5 or 10 devices. Check your plan details in your Norton account.

Q2: What if my product key isn’t working?

Double-check the characters—product keys can be case-sensitive. If the issue persists, contact Norton Support for assistance.

Q3: I already have Norton installed—can I just enter a new key?

Yes. Open Norton, go to Help > Enter Product Key, and input the new code. This is also useful when you need to reinstall Norton with a product key.

Q4: Is it safe to download Norton from websites other than www.norton.com/setup?

No. Always use official Norton websites such as my.norton.com home setup or www.norton.com/setup to avoid malware or fake software.
